Popular Dishes

Grilled Kalamata Chicken

Savor our signature Kalamata Chicken, a juicy grilled chicken breast topped with tangy feta cheese, fresh tomatoes, and briny Kalamata olives. Served with seasoned rice, roasted potatoes, and a medley of fresh vegetables.

Pork & Chicken Gyro

Enjoy the best of both worlds with our pork and chicken gyro, made with tender, marinated meats slow-roasted on the spit and wrapped in soft pita bread. Topped with fresh tomatoes, onions, and creamy tzatziki.

Horiatiki Greek Salad

Our classic Horiatiki Greek Salad is made with ripe tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, red onions, green peppers, and creamy feta cheese. Light, refreshing, and full of flavour.

Naxos Fish Plate

Our Naxos Fish Plate features a crispy golden fillet of fish, served with fresh lemon, Paréa salad, seasoned rice, and roasted lemon-garlic potatoes. A perfect balance of crisp, zesty, and hearty flavours.

Calamari

Crispy, golden, and full of flavour — our Calamari is lightly breaded, perfectly seasoned, and served with cool, creamy tzatziki. This classic Greek appetizer is the perfect start to any meal.

Our Story

Welcome to Parea Orléans, where every dish is crafted with love, tradition, and the freshest ingredients. Our family recipes come straight from Greece, perfected over generations and brought to life in the heart of Orléans.

From tender souvlaki and homemade dips to fresh salads and grilled seafood, every plate reflects our passion for authentic Greek cuisine. At Parea, you can dine in, order take-out, or enjoy delivery, all while experiencing the same warm hospitality we'd offer at our family table.

Whether you're joining us for a casual meal, a special celebration, or simply craving Greek comfort food at home, we're here to make every bite memorable.
