The Parea Experience

At Parea, we believe food is about more than a meal — it's about sharing warmth, tradition, and the authentic flavours of Greece with our community here in Ottawa. Our family recipes, perfected over generations, are crafted with the freshest ingredients to bring you a true taste of Greece.

With two locations, we make it easy to enjoy our cuisine your way: Parea in Orléans offers dine-in, pickup, delivery, catering, and online ordering, while Parea Express on Montréal Road focuses exclusively on quick take-out meals prepared with the same quality and care. Whether you're gathering with family, ordering from home, or picking up something on the go, Parea is here to bring people together around great food.
