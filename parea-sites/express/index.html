Parea Express Story

Life moves fast, but great food should always be simple and delicious. That's why we created Parea Express on Montreal Road, Ottawa — a quick-service Greek counter offering take-out only.

From warm gyro wraps and crisp salads to hearty platters, everything is made with the same high-quality ingredients and family recipes that Parea is known for.

Parea Express is perfect for:

  • Busy lunch breaks
  • Easy family dinners
  • Grabbing a fresh meal on your way home
  • Quick authentic Greek food without compromise

With take-out and online ordering, we make it easy to enjoy authentic Greek flavours without slowing down your day.
